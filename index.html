Feel the Force

Contact-Driven Learning from Humans

Abstract

FEELTHEFORCE (FTF) is a robot learning system that models human tactile behavior to learn force-sensitive manipulation. Using a tactile glove and vision-based pose estimation, we train a closed-loop policy for fine-grained force control on a Franka Panda robot. FTF achieves a 75% success rate across multiple manipulation tasks, offering robust, scalable human supervision for tactile learning.

Key Features

  • Tactile glove for real human force data collection
  • Transformer-based force prediction policy
  • PD controller for real-time force tracking
  • Zero-shot transfer to a Franka Panda robot
